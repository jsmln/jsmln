### ⚡ ATLAS: An LLM-Driven Instructional Management Platform

*Automated Syllabus-to-Exam Generation and Prescriptive Assessment Analytics*

</div>

> A **multi-tenant PaaS** for Philippine HEIs that automates the full OBE compliance workflow —
> from CHED-aligned syllabus generation through TOS-validated exam construction to prescriptive
> analytics — cutting faculty prep time from **7+ hours to under 20 minutes** per course.

<br/>

|:---|:---|

| 📝 **Assessment Engine** | TOS-computed, Bloom's-mapped MCQ, essay & mixed exams with auto answer keys |
| 📊 **Score Analytics** | Retrieves weighted grades directly from faculty Google Sheets via OAuth 2.0 |
